More About Me

I was born on July 3rd, 2002. Growing up I moved around with my family a lot. We have finally settled down in Baldwyn, MS, and then I moved out to go to BaseCamp Coding Academy to follow my dreams. I've wanted to be a computer programmer or software developer (really anything to do with computers) since I was 11 years old.

Before BaseCamp I attended college at Itawamba Community College and learned Visual Basic, Html and CSS, as well as learned the foundation and working parts of any computer in my IT Foundations class. I managed to snag a work study during college to help me get the feel of a work environment. All of this has helped me learn more efficiently and more quickly when I came to BaseCamp.

Seeing as I have started BaseCamp, I am now proficient in Python, HTML and CSS, as well as Visual Basic. I cannot wait to learn more languages as well.

I am still attending BaseCamp, and will learn Bootstrap, Javascript, Java, and Django.

I soon hope to obtain an internship from a company that will further help me develop my skills and allow me to improve to better help the company.
